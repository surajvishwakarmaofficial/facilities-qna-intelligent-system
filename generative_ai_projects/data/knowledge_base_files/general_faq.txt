Q: What are the standard office hours?
A: The building is accessible to employees 24/7 via their access badge. Standard business hours for guest reception and administrative support are from 8:30 AM to 6:00 PM, Monday to Friday.

Q: Where can I find the mailroom?
A: The mailroom is located on the 1st floor, past the main reception area and to the left. Mail is typically sorted and delivered to department mail slots by 11:00 AM daily.

Q: Is there a Lost and Found?
A: Yes, the Lost and Found is managed by the security desk on the 1st floor. If you find an item, please bring it there. If you have lost an item, you can check with the guard on duty.

Q: What is the policy on kitchen use?
A: Please clean up after yourself in the kitchen areas. Label any food you place in the shared refrigerators with your name and the date. Unlabeled food is removed every Friday evening.
