**InnovateCorp Official Guest & Visitor Policy**
Effective Date: January 1, 2024

**1. Visitor Registration**
All guests must be pre-registered by their employee host using the internal 'Visitor Welcome' portal. Upon arrival, all guests must check in at the front desk located on the 1st floor. They will be required to present a valid photo ID to receive their visitor badge.

**2. Host Responsibilities**
The employee host is responsible for their guest at all times. Guests must be escorted in all areas of the building and are not permitted in secure zones without prior authorization. The host must escort their guest out of the building upon the conclusion of their visit.

**3. Wi-Fi Access**
A guest Wi-Fi network named "InnovateCorp_Guest" is available. The password can be obtained from the front desk upon successful check-in and is also printed on the back of the visitor badge.

**4. Working Hours**
Standard guest access is permitted between 8:30 AM and 6:00 PM on weekdays. Access outside of these hours requires written approval from the department head and must be submitted to building security 24 hours in advance.
